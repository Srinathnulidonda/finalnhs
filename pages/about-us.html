Our History

Established in 2004, Nalanda High School has grown from humble beginnings to become one of the most respected educational institutions in Armoor. Named after the ancient seat of learning in Bihar, our school embodies the same principles of academic excellence and holistic development.

Over the years, we have continuously evolved our educational approach to meet the changing needs of our students while maintaining our commitment to academic rigor and character development.

Our Mission

At Nalanda High School, our mission is to provide a stimulating and supportive environment where students can discover and develop their unique talents and abilities. We aim to nurture well-rounded individuals who are academically proficient, socially responsible, and morally upright.

Our Vision

We envision Nalanda High School as a center of educational excellence that prepares students to face the challenges of the 21st century with confidence and competence. We strive to create global citizens who contribute positively to society while respecting diversity and upholding ethical values.

Why Choose Us?

Academic Excellence

Our curriculum is designed to challenge students and foster critical thinking skills. Our consistently high academic results reflect our commitment to excellence.

Individual Attention

With a favorable teacher-student ratio, we ensure that each student receives personalized attention and guidance to achieve their full potential.

Experienced Faculty

Our dedicated team of teachers brings a wealth of experience and expertise to the classroom, inspiring and motivating students to excel.

We are dedicated to providing quality education and nurturing the potential of every student in a safe and supportive environment.
